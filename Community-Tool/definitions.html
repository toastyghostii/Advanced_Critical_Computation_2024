Definitions


Asexual

Someone who experiences little to no sexual attraction


A-spec/Ace-Spec

Someone who falls somewhere on the asexual spectrum/is not allosexual


Allosexual

Someone who regularly experiences sexual attraction


Graysexual/Gray-Asexual

Someone who experiences little or limited/circumstansial sexual attraction


Demisexual

Someone who only experiences sexual attraction towards someone they have a strong emotional bond with


Aegosexual

Someone who feels a disconnect between themself and the subject of arousal


Lithosexual

Someone who experiences sexual attraction, but doesn't want or that attraction may go away if it is reciprocated


Aromantic

Someone who experiences little to no romantic attraction



This is a limited list of common terms used within the asexual community. For a more comprehensive list of terminology, refer to the Asexuality Handbook

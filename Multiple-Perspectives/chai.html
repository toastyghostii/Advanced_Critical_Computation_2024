Rituals

For many, tea drinking is a ritual done daily. Tea is an important part of many cultures. Many South Asians have chai-- black tea with milk, and spices like ginger, cloves or cardamom-- two or even three times a day. Despite the caffeine, it can be a relaxing experience for people who have developed tea drinking as a ritual.
